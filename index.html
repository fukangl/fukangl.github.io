I am a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University. I am co-advised by Prof. Zackory Erikson and Prof. Zeynep Temel.

Before coming to CMU, I spent time at Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, where I worked with Prof. Bin Fang and Prof. Fuchun Sun. I graduated from Tsinghua University with a M.S. in Mechanics, and from Tongji Unviersity with a B.S. in Engineering Mechanics. Previously, I My research interests lie in robotics, machine learning, and reinforcement learning. My current research focuses on assistive robotics.
